Under Pressure

"Under Pressure" is a song by the British rock band Queen and the British singer David Bowie. It was released as a single in 1981 and later included on Queen's 1982 album "Hot Space." The song is known for its distinctive bassline, powerful vocals, and socially conscious lyrics. It addresses themes of stress, pressure, and the challenges of modern life. "Under Pressure" has become one of Queen and David Bowie's most iconic collaborations and remains a popular and influential track in rock music history.
